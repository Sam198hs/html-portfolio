Favourite Movies in 2024

Amaran

Amaran, is a powerful cinematic tribute to the life and sacrifices of Major Mukund Varadarajan, a brave Indian Army officer who laid down his life in the line of duty. Directed by Rajkumar Periaswamy, the film skillfully weaves together the personal and professional facets of Major Mukund's life, offering a poignant and inspiring tale.
